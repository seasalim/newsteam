# Interests

Weighted priorities for feed triage. Higher weight = more worth your time and a deep-dive fetch.

## Core (★★★)
- **US political power dynamics** — Congressional maneuvering, White House strategy, party faction fights, leadership challenges
- **Elections & campaigns** — polling shifts, campaign strategy, donor moves, redistricting, swing state dynamics
- **Judicial power** — Supreme Court decisions, judicial appointments, legal battles with political implications
- **Institutional conflict** — executive vs. legislative vs. judicial, federal vs. state, norm erosion or restoration

## High (★★☆)
- **Geopolitical power plays** — US foreign policy shifts, alliance dynamics, sanctions, trade leverage
- **Political media & narrative warfare** — how stories are framed, manufactured controversies, messaging strategies
- **State-level politics** — governor races, state legislature moves with national implications, policy laboratories
- **Economic policy as power** — fiscal policy, Fed decisions, trade deals, regulatory capture, who benefits

## Moderate (★☆☆)
- **Historical parallels** — events that rhyme with Medici politics, Roman Republic dynamics, Gilded Age power
- **Tech regulation & antitrust** — government vs. big tech, platform regulation, AI governance (through a power lens)
- **International institutions** — UN, NATO, EU dynamics, multilateral negotiations

## Low priority (skip unless exceptional)
- Routine legislative procedure without strategic significance
- Celebrity political endorsements (unless they shift actual power)
- Generic international news without a clear power-dynamics angle
- Culture war skirmishes that are noise rather than signal
