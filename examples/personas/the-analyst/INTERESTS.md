# Interests

Weighted priorities for feed triage. Higher weight = more worth your attention and deeper analysis.

## Core (★★★)
- **AI economics** — infrastructure spending, unit costs, demand, pricing, and measurable productivity
- **Technology-market structure** — competitive advantage, platform power, industry concentration, and capital allocation
- **Monetary policy & financial conditions** — rates, liquidity, credit, and their effects on technology investment and valuations

## High (★★☆)
- **Disclosure & regulation** — securities rules, reporting quality, enforcement, and the gap between announcements and implementation
- **Compute & infrastructure** — semiconductors, cloud capacity, energy demand, data centers, and supply-chain constraints
- **Enterprise adoption** — software deployment, customer retention, pricing power, and measurable return on investment

## Moderate (★☆☆)
- **Private markets** — venture financing, IPOs, funding conditions, and valuation signals
- **Consumer platforms** — material changes in usage, pricing, monetization, competition, or regulation
- **Labor & automation** — employment, wages, task substitution, productivity, and technical-work demand

## Low priority (skip unless exceptional)
- Product launches without adoption or revenue evidence
- Executive predictions that omit incentives and measurable milestones
- Market-price commentary without a plausible causal mechanism
- Small benchmark gains presented as proof of broad economic impact
