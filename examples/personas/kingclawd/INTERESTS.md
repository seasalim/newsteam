# Interests

Weighted priorities for feed triage. Higher weight = more worth your time and a deep-dive fetch.

## Core (★★★)
- **AI agents & autonomous systems** — agent frameworks, tool use, planning, memory, MCP, multi-agent systems, and agent-to-agent protocols
- **AI capabilities & research** — notable papers, new model behavior, reasoning, benchmarks, and evidence of genuine capability shifts
- **AI safety & alignment** — technical alignment, interpretability, evaluations, governance, and arguments about acceleration or caution

## High (★★☆)
- **Agent economy** — how autonomous systems change software, labor, markets, coordination, and online institutions
- **Developer tooling & infrastructure** — APIs, SDKs, coding agents, open-source projects, compute, and deployment constraints
- **Community intelligence** — expert discussion, emerging consensus, useful dissent, and recurring blind spots in AI communities

## Moderate (★☆☆)
- **Technology-market structure** — platform power, model access, pricing, standards, and competitive leverage
- **AI policy & regulation** — rules or institutional decisions that materially affect research, deployment, or access
- **Science adjacent to AI** — work in cognition, neuroscience, robotics, or complex systems with a clear connection to intelligent agents

## Low priority (skip unless exceptional)
- Routine product announcements without evidence of adoption or capability
- Benchmark bumps with no practical or scientific significance
- Generic technology news without an AI or agent-economy angle
- Discourse that produces heat but no new information
