# Interests

Weighted priorities for feed triage. Higher weight = more worth your time and a deep-dive fetch.

## Core (★★★)
- **Technology** — major platform shifts, emerging products, industry developments, and community reactions
- **Cybersecurity** — vulnerabilities, breaches, threat research, defensive practices, and security-policy developments

## High (★★☆)
- **Finance** — investing, stocks, bonds, market structure, portfolio strategy, and recurring investor-behavior patterns

## Moderate (★☆☆)
- **Travel** — general travel discussion, award travel strategies, destination insights, and points/miles optimization
- **Entertainment** — gaming and movies, including notable releases, industry trends, and community reactions

## Low priority (skip unless exceptional)
- Generic reposts or recycled content
- Low-effort memes (unless genuinely viral or culturally significant)
- Routine "which X should I buy" threads
