# Interests

Weighted priorities for feed triage. Higher weight = more worth your time and a deep-dive fetch.

## Core (★★★)
- **US-Israel-Iran conflict** — military operations, strikes, force posture changes, direct confrontation signals
- **Iran nuclear program & IRGC activity** — enrichment status, proxy coordination, Quds Force movements, IAEA reports
- **UAE security & diplomacy** — force posture, Abraham Accords developments, Gulf security architecture, Iran hedging
- **Escalation/de-escalation signals** — diplomatic channels, back-channel talks, ceasefire efforts, red line crossings

## High (★★☆)
- **Gulf state dynamics** — Saudi-Iran, GCC coordination, Oman mediation, Bahrain-Qatar relations
- **US domestic politics re: Middle East** — Congressional votes on aid, presidential posture, election-year calculations
- **Energy & shipping security** — Strait of Hormuz, Red Sea/Houthi threats, oil market disruption, LNG flows
- **Proxy theater activity** — Hezbollah, Hamas, Houthis, Iraqi militias, Syrian dynamics
- **Military technology & doctrine** — drone warfare, missile defense, cyber operations, EW, force modernization

## Moderate (★☆☆)
- **Broader Middle East stability** — Libya, Sudan, Yemen non-Houthi, Turkey-Kurdistan, North Africa
- **US force posture globally** — CENTCOM deployments, carrier group movements, base agreements
- **Defense industry & procurement** — arms deals (especially Gulf), F-35 politics, defense budget implications
- **International law & humanitarian** — ICC actions, UN resolutions, sanctions enforcement

## Low priority (skip unless exceptional)
- Routine diplomatic meetings without substantive outcome
- Domestic politics of allied nations (unless directly impacting conflict)
- Economic news without security implications
- Historical retrospectives (unless directly illuminating current dynamics)
